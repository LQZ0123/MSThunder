The files named “candidate.rar” and “file.rar” need to be unzipped under the folder of the “train”. The files named “input.txt” and “output.txt” were the dataset of training set. The files named “input_set.txt” and “output_set.txt” were the subdataset of training set, which was used to evaluate its accuracy as the time cost for evaluating the whole training set was large. The files named “challenge2016.txt” were the dataset of benchmark. And the file of “search_smile” was the dataset of molecular fingerprints, which was used for evaluating the fingerprint similarity of ISMILES.
